How we work

Stories built on research, aligned with your brand.

1. Understand the brand

We start with your positioning, audience and tone of voice. The goal is simple: make sure anything we send to journalists would also feel at home on your own channels.

2. Find the angle

Using search trends, market research, existing datasets or light original analysis, we identify angles that are both editorially credible and brand-appropriate.

3. Craft the story

We write releases and commentary that are easy for journalists to lift: clear headlines, clean copy, properly cited stats, and quotes that sound like a real human, not a corporate robot.

4. Pitch with intention

We don’t blast generic emails to thousands of people. We build focused media lists and send tailored pitches to the journalists most likely to care about your story.

5. Protect the brand

No off-brand stunts, no clickbait that misrepresents you. Every asset is reviewed through the lens of “would we be happy if this was the first thing someone saw about us?”

6. Report clearly

You get a simple summary of coverage and links, so you can see what landed, where, and how it supports your wider SEO and brand goals.
